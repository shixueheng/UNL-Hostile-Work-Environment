# UNL-Hostile-Work-Environment
This repository documents the data regarding the elimination of the Department of Statistics at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln (UNL), as well as a Title IX-related matter happened inside UNL Statistics.

The materials collected here focus on issues of academic freedom, freedom of expression, shared governance, procedural fairness, and administrative decision-making. They include records, correspondence, and other documentation related to the department elimination process and my interactions with university administrators and faculty members.

The purpose of this repository is to preserve relevant information, promote transparency, and provide a factual record that allows others to independently review the events and draw their own conclusions.

All views and opinions expressed in this repository are my own.
